# Personas (Narrative Detail)

### 1. Frequent Traveler
High-value cohort with repeat bookings and low cancellation rates.
Booking patterns exhibit weekday bias and moderate browsing duration.
Economically attractive segment for loyalty status and upgrade perks.

Behavioral hypothesis:
- low elasticity
- responds to status signaling + priority perks

### 2. Deal Seeker
Price-sensitive users with high discount-affinity and flexible dates.
Longer browsing windows and more comparisons.

Behavioral hypothesis:
- high elasticity
- convert on marginal discount stimuli

### 3. Canceller
Segment characterized by uncertainty-driven drop-off.
High cancellation counts suggest volatile travel intent.

Behavioral hypothesis:
- high perceived booking risk
- responds to flexibility-enhancing perks (free cancel, rebook)
